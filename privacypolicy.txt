Privacy Policy for Niftons Arena

CASSIA TRADE Ltd ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use our multiplayer VR game called Niftons Arena ("the game").

Personal Information We Collect
When you use the game, we get the following personal information from you:

    Oculus username: This is used to show your name during the multiplayer match.
    Device information: This is used for the backend services and crash reporting.

How We Use Your Personal Information
We do not store any personal information on our servers. The personal information is only used by our third-party service providers, including Meta, Photon Engine, and Unity Gaming Services, when you are using the game, to provide the game and related services. We do not share your personal information with any other third parties, except as required by law.

Security and Protection of User Data
We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, and disclosure. However, no security measures are perfect and we cannot guarantee the security of your personal information.

Access and Management of Personal Information
Since we do not store any personal information, there is no account for you to manage. If you want to uninstall the game, you can do so at any time.

Legal Bases for Processing User Data and User Rights
The legal bases for processing your personal information may include your consent or our legitimate interests in providing and improving the game and related services. You have the right to request access to and rectification or erasure of your personal information, as well as the right to object to or restrict certain processing of your personal information. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies
We do not use cookies on our website or in the game. However, cookies and other tracking technologies may be used by our third-party service providers, including Oculus, Photon Engine, and Unity Gaming Services, as part of their SDKs. Please refer to their respective privacy policies for more information.

Changes to this Privacy Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated Privacy Policy will be posted on our website and will be effective as of the date of posting.
