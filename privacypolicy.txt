Privacy Policy for Niftons Arena

David Riha ("I", "me"), a sole proprietor based in the Czech Republic, am committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how I collect, use, and share information when you use my multiplayer VR game called Niftons Arena ("the game").

Personal Information I Collect

When you use the game, I collect the following personal information from you:

Oculus/PICO username: This is displayed as your name during the multiplayer match.
Device information: This is used for backend services and crash reporting.
User ID and list of friends: This is used for sending App Invites within the app, but is not stored on my servers.
How I Use Your Personal Information

I do not store any personal information on my servers. Your personal information is only used by my third-party service providers, including Meta, Photon Engine, and Unity Gaming Services, when you are using the game, to provide the game and related services. I do not share your personal information with any other third parties, except as required by law.

Security and Protection of User Data

I take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, and disclosure. However, no security measures are perfect, and I cannot guarantee the security of your personal information.

Access and Management of Personal Information

Since I do not store any personal information, there is no account for you to manage. If you want to uninstall the game, you can do so at any time.

Legal Bases for Processing User Data and User Rights

The legal bases for processing your personal information may include your consent or my legitimate interests in providing and improving the game and related services. You have the right to request access to and rectification or erasure of your personal information, as well as the right to object to or restrict certain processing of your personal information. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

I do not use cookies on our website or in the game. However, cookies and other tracking technologies may be used by our third-party service providers, including Oculus, PICO, Photon Engine, and Unity Gaming Services, as part of their SDKs. Please refer to their respective privacy policies for more information.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

I may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated Privacy Policy will be posted on my website and will be effective as of the date of posting.
